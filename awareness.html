What this campaign is about:




This is an awareness campaign to aware poor people of government schemes that are to help them but they never get to know about such schemes due to lack of knowledge and
Here comes our work to let them aware of these

Some government schemes are mentioned below in this document. All the details about schemes are in there.




What we did:



First we visited some poor people.

And then we talk to them about the schemes given in the document below.

And told them every benefits of these schemes and how it can benefit them.

We also told them if they are eligible for that scheme or not.

If they were, we told them how to apply for that scheme or where they can apply for that scheme .

For those who are unable to apply we did that on behalf of them .
