Clothes Distribution



November 5, 2020 No Comments


An epidemic like Corona has added to the woes of the poor. In view of this, the team of Helping Hut organization came forward in some areas of Gwalior. They distributed old clothes making a fair distance to poor and helpless families. It is our duty to help such needy poor families and illuminate their dark world. This will not only make them happy, but will also make us happy. By donating old clothes, blankets, we can provide them happiness as a gift on this Diwali . This quota of ours can make their Diwali special.
