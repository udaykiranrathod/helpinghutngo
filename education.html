Education Kit Distribution



September 3, 2020 No Comments





“Education makes people easy to lead, but difficult to drive; Easy to govern but impossible to enslave.”







So many children in india and around the world are deprived of education which is the basic right of everychildren. sometimes even if they get the access to school they don’t have proper stationary to practice and learn.

We came up with the idea of distribution of education kit of 50 rupees each which includes books,copies, pencils and needed things…which was distributed on 15th August (Independence Day) in account to provide a hope that nothing should come as a barrier in their studies and shaping their life.
